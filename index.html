Hi, I am Mehdi
​Welcome to my personal webpage!
I am a data scientist with proven expertise in applying mathematical statistics and predictive algorithms to solve complex problems for finance, engineering, environment and academia. I have a PhD degree in Geostatistics from University of Alberta, Canada. Geostatistics is a branch of statistics focusing ​on spatial or spatiotemporal datasets. Currently work as data scientist at Symend, a leading SaaS company. Prior that, I was an instructor and researcher at University of Calgary. I have posted some of my past projects, lectures, and publications. Feel free to take a look at my website.

Experience

1- Sep 2020 - Present  
Data Scientist at Symend, Calgary, Canada.

  • ● Exploratory data analysis, data mining, feature engineering and data visualization for large amounts of structured and unstructured data using scientific methods and algorithms.
  • ● Apply mathematical theory by developing tools such as boosted models, decision trees, dimensionality reduction, time series forecasting, NLP, cluster analysis, feature selection, experimental design, and other techniques.
  • ● Research and evaluate advanced machine learning techniques to analyze statistically derived tests and develop relevant, timely, targeted outreaches to grow business knowledge and improve KPIs.
  • ● Provide mentorship and leadership to data science teams and lead research and development of machine learning, advanced statistical techniques and mathematical analyses.


2- Mar 2018 - Jan 2021
Data Scientist at University of Calgary, Calgary, Canada.

  • ● Data manipulation, feature importance, data visualization, and clustering analysis to detect patterns and anomalies in acquired data from satellite and field monitoring tools for greenhouse gas emissions.
  • ● Lead research and development of statistical rigour and machine learning to measure greenhouse gas emissions and prioritize amendment of leakiest hydrocarbon wells to avoid environmental pollution.
  • ● Sessional instructor for two courses: 1- Machine Learning for Energy System (ENCH 619.78), 2- Advanced Geostatistics (GLGY 697), requiring course materials preparation, teaching and supervising students.
  • ● Monitor model drift by frequently measure model performance and compare features using statistical tests and update model as needed.


3- Sep 2013 - Mar 2018  
 Geostatistician at Centre for Computational Geostatistics, Edmonton, Canada.

  • ● Research and develop geostatistical analysis to improve subsurface resource estimation in case of limited data by quantifying reliable uncertainty in input statistics and declustering for preferential sampling and sampling bias.
  • ● Enhance the performance of predictive algorithms to address challenges for energy companies to make reliable decision for drilling a new well.
  • ● Technical writing and communication skills achieved through teaching, presentations, writing research proposals, publishing peer-reviewed and conference papers.


4- Jan 2010 to Sep 2013   
 Geosphysicist at Dana Energy, Iran.

  • ● Plan and conduct hydrocarbon exploration by seismic survey, data management and data visualization of field acquisition for interpretation.
  • ● Data manipulation, quality control and processing of big survey data for optimum drilling location to maximize hydrocarbon production.
